The Pittsburgh Queer History Project (PQHP) is an oral history and media initiative, focusing on LGBTQ after-hours nightlife in Pittsburgh, PA from 1960 to 1990. The PQHP’s mission is to develop robust record of these communities through a combination of community centered events and home preservation education.
